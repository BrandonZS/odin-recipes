Baked Ziki

This is the best baked Ziki recipe ever!!

This baked ziti is always a hit! A lady I worked with brought this in one day, and everyone loved it. Now it is the favorite of all my dinner guests. I have made this also without the meat, and it is well received.

    Ingredients

  • Dry ziti pasta
  • Onion
  • Lean ground beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Provolone cheese

    Steps

  1. Cook ziti pasta
  2. Brown beef and onion, stir spaghetti sauce
  3. Preheat oven 350 degrees
  4. Spread ziti and cover with all ingredients
  5. Bake for 30 minutes
